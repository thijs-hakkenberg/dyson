### Phase 3 - Matroska Brain
# Phase 3 - Matroska Brain Architecture
## Technical Proposal

The transition from a simple energy-harvesting Dyson Swarm to a Matroska Brain represents a shift from energy generation to energy *utilization* at the source. This phase constructs nested shells of computational substrate, where each outer shell is powered by the waste heat of the inner shell. The goal is to maximize the Landau limit of computational efficiency for the entire solar system.

The architecture assumes the Phase 2 swarm (at ~0.5-0.8 AU) becomes the "Hot Layer" (Layer 1). Phase 3 focuses on building the cooler, outer computational layers and the inter-layer connectivity.
